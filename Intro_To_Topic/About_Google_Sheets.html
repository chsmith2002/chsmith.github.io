About Google Sheets

Google Sheets is part of the Google Suite. It is a free cloud-based spreadsheet software that allows for users to work on spreadsheets simultaneously with a team, or for personal use. Google Sheets can be used to create databases, track finances, etc. Sheets allows users to create, format, and work on spreadsheets through standard manual entry, or by utilizing functions. It is often compared to Microsoft Excel, but is considered to be more user-friendly and accessible.
